# 💡 How to Ask AI About Your Notes

## Quick Start

1. **Open any document** in your notebook
2. **Switch to 📝 Text view** (top-right toggle)
3. **Highlight any text** (at least 10 characters)
4. **Click "Ask AI ✨"** when the bubble appears
5. **Get instant explanations!**

---

## Desktop Instructions

### Step 1: Select Text
Click and drag across the text you want explained:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ Photosynthesis is the process   │
│ by which plants convert light   │ ← Click and drag
│ energy into chemical energy...  │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 2: Release Mouse
A glowing bubble appears above your selection:

```
        ┌─────────────┐
        │ Ask AI ✨   │
        └──────┬──────┘
               │
┌──────────────▼──────────────────┐
│ Photosynthesis is the process   │
│ by which plants convert light   │
│ energy into chemical energy...  │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 3: Click the Bubble
AI explains your selection in the chat panel:

```
You: Explain this: "Photosynthesis is..."

AI: Photosynthesis is a fundamental biological 
    process where plants use sunlight to...
```

## Mobile Instructions

### Step 1: Long Press
Tap and hold on text, then drag to select:

```
📱 Long press → Drag → Lift finger
```

### Step 2: Tap the Bubble
The "Ask AI ✨" bubble appears above selection

### Step 3: Get Explanation
AI responds in the chat panel below

## Tips & Tricks

### ✅ Best Practices

- **Select complete sentences** for better context
- **Minimum 10 characters** required
- **Use Text view** for highlighting (Document view has limitations)
- **AI uses full document** for context, not just selection

### 🎯 What to Ask

Good selections:
- Technical terms: "mitochondria"
- Complex concepts: "quantum entanglement"  
- Full sentences: "The derivative represents..."
- Formulas: "E = mc²"
- Definitions: "A prime number is..."

## Troubleshooting

### Bubble doesn't appear

**Problem**: Selected text too short  
**Solution**: Select at least 10 characters

**Problem**: In Document view (PDF/DOCX)  
**Solution**: Switch to 📝 Text view

**Problem**: Clicked outside selection  
**Solution**: Selection cleared - try again

### AI doesn't understand

**Problem**: Selection lacks context  
**Solution**: Select more text or full sentence

**Problem**: Technical jargon  
**Solution**: AI has document context - it should understand

## Examples

### Example 1: Define a Term

**You select**: "photosynthesis"

**AI explains**:
> Photosynthesis is the process by which plants, algae, and some bacteria convert light energy (usually from the sun) into chemical energy stored in glucose. It occurs in chloroplasts and involves two main stages: light-dependent reactions and the Calvin cycle.

### Example 2: Explain a Concept

**You select**: "The derivative of a function at a point represents the slope of the tangent line at that point."

**AI explains**:
> This statement captures the geometric interpretation of derivatives in calculus. When you take the derivative of a function f(x) at a specific point, you're finding the instantaneous rate of change at that point. Visually, this corresponds to the slope of a line that just touches the curve at that single point - the tangent line.

### Example 3: Simplify Complex Text

**You select**: "The mitochondrial electron transport chain couples oxidative phosphorylation to ATP synthesis via chemiosmotic coupling."

**AI explains**:
> Let me break this down: Your cells have tiny power plants called mitochondria. Inside them, there's a chain of proteins that moves electrons around. As electrons move through this chain, they pump hydrogen ions, creating an electrical gradient. This gradient powers an enzyme that makes ATP - your cell's energy currency. It's like a waterfall turning a turbine to generate electricity.

## Feature Limits

- **Minimum**: 10 characters
- **Context**: First 5,000 characters of document sent to AI
- **Token cost**: 5 tokens per chat message
- **Rate limit**: 10 requests per minute

---

## Privacy

✅ Your selections are **never stored**  
✅ Only sent to AI when you click "Ask AI"  
✅ Processed in real-time, not saved  
✅ Document content stays private
